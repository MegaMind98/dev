Rules and Regulations

Participants must agree upon the below mentioned rules and regulations:

The game will be played in Captain Draft mode.

First Warning: The team captain has to acknowledge the first warning.

Second Warning: This will lead to forfeiting of the game.

Third Warning: Third warning will lead to disqualification from the entire tournament.

All teams have to provide the administration the following information upon request:

Full Names of all team members.

Steam accounts of all team members.

Primary nicknames of all team members.

Nationality of all team members.

Identification of the Captain and/or of the manager.

Clan Logo (100 x 100px or bigger).

Skype account of the captain and/or manager.
