Project Exhibition

Show your project at NEXT 2019.

Project Exhibition at NEXT 2019 includes hardware, software, civil projects demonstration where all the participants would be judged on the basis of their idea, presentation skill and group work.

Participants can present their respective hardware, software projects and civil models separately in the exhibition.


Entry Fee

The entry fee per team is NPR 2,000
Maximum of 4 members are allowed in a team(for Civil Project 6 members are allowed).


Awards and Recognition

Winning team of any category will be awarded with following cash prize:

  • First prize: NPR 8,000
  • Second prize: NPR 5,000
  • Young and innovative idea (+2 level students): NPR 5,000
  • People Choice Award: NPR 5000

All the participants will be awarded with a certificate of participation.

The winning team will be awarded with trophy as well as medals for all the team members.


Team Specification

A team may consist of maximum of four members. (For Civil, Minimum: 4; Maximum: 6)

Students from engineering and IT background can only participate in the exhibition.

Collaboration from different colleges are not allowed for competition but they can showcase their projects.


Rules and Regulations

  • The project should be completed before the time of demonstration.
  • Theme of the project should be specified at the time of registration.
  • Report should be submitted on time(before 5pm of 26th of Ashadh )
  • Participants are allowed to use any programming languages.
  • At the time of judging, all the members of the team should be present.
  • Dress code of the participants should be formal.
  • Decision of Jury Panel would be final.
  • Project description should be submitted with this form.

Deadline

The deadline for form submission is July 11 , 2019.
