Venue

FIFA 19 along with all the activities of the NEXT 2019 will be organized at NCIT College, Balkumari. Please refer to the map or contact the resource person if you need any help in finding the location.


Entry Fee

The entry fee for the registration at FIFA is NPR 500 per person.


Prizes and a Certificates

1st Prize: NPR 6,000 + Football Jersey of his/her choice
2nd Prize: NPR 4,000 + Football Jersey of his/her choice
3rd Prize: NPR 2,000 + Football Jersey of his/her choice


Rules and Regulations

Participants must agree upon the below mentioned rules and regulations:

  • Use of abusive language is strictly prohibited.
  • Controllers will be provided as per the preference of the player. Each player will be responsible for the consoles and controllers they are provided with.
  • All matches will be best of 1 game.
  • No warm-up or practice games are permitted once the first game has begun.
  • If a match is played after the scheduled time, it will not be considered as a warm-up and the result of the game will be counted as an official result.
  • If a player remains disconnected for the first 30 second or the first goal is scored while 1 player remains disconnected, the game must be restarted otherwise the game will be resumed at the closest possible point.
  • Breaking any rule and any form of cheating, glitching, abusing in the game mechanics or unsportsmanlike behavior may result in a forfeit of a game.
  • The player may not use a turbo controller, a button macro controller or keyboard/mouse.
