Rapid Coding

Rapid Coding Tournament in NEXT is targeted towards students and code enthusiasts with an elementary understanding of programming.

Rapid Coding Format

Rapid coding tournament is the tournament amidst groups of candidates, each containing 2 members.

The coding tournament will be divided into the number of rounds. In each round, the teams will be divided into slots of 4 or fewer teams and they will compete with each other by solving the given programming question.

Logical accuracy of the program and time exercised in solving will play a significant role in selecting the winners of each round.

The complexity level of the question increases with the number of rounds.

In this competition, time and space complexity of the program or algorithm won’t be taken into consideration.

Entry Fee

The entry fee per team is NRs 200/-

Maximum of 2 members are allowed in a team.

Prizes and a Certificates

First Prize: Rs.6000/- and a Certificate

Certificate of appreciation for 2nd and 3rd runner up.

Rules and Regulations

Participants must agree upon the below mentioned rules and regulations:

Participant must bring their own laptop and charger.

There will be no internet connection during the competition.

Copy and pen for the participant will be provided.

For any issues arrived participants must agree upon the jury’s decision.

Collaboration among team member is expected but one team can’t collaborate with other teams during competition.

The competition will be according to the tie-sheet.
