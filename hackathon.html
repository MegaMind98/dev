NEXT Hackathon

NEXT Hackathon is a competitive platform aiming to generate innovative solutions to real-world problems in a risk-taking environment.

Hackathon Format

NEXT hackathon is scheduled into 3 days as follows:

Day 1

- Idea Presentation

- Research, idea mentoring and implementation

Day 2

- Implementation and mentoring

Day 3

- Presentation

- Prize distribution

Entry Fee

The entry fee per team is NRs 2000/-

Maximum of 4 members are allowed in a team.

Prizes and Certificates

Winner : Rs 20,000/- and a Certificate

Runner Up: Rs 10,000/- and a Certificate

Best Idea: Rs 3000/- and a Certificate

Best Presentation: Rs 3000/- and a Certificate

Certificate of appreciation will be given to all the participants.

Date and Time

Date: 18th July 2019 - 20th July 2019

Time: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM ( 18th and 19th July)

7:30 AM - 12:30 AM(20th july)
