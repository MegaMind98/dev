NEXT 2019

July 18–20, 2019 @ NCIT
Brewing innovative minds.


NCIT EXPO ON TECHNOLOGY “NEXT” will be the first ever nationwide fest on IT organized by Nepal College of Information and Technology. This event involves an active participation of students from more than 37 affiliated colleges of Pokhara University, +2 colleges within the valley and other engineering colleges.
