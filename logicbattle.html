Logic Battle

Show your logical and programming command.

Logic Battle Format

Logic Battle is going to hold on the second day of NEXT fest.

It is the competition between groups of candidates, each containing 2-3 members. Each group will be assigned to solve a few programming questions in the specific time limit of 3 hours.

The winner of this competition will be selected by the experts on the basis of the flow of logic (algorithm) and output of the respective programs.

Time and Space complexity of the algorithm will also be taken into consideration.


Entry Fee

The entry fee per team is NPR 300
Maximum of 3 members are allowed in a team.


Date and Time

Date: July 19, 2019 (Second day of NEXT)
Time: 10:00 AM


Prizes and a Certificates

First Prize: NPR 9,000 + $100 credit of Amazon Web Services (AWS) + Certificate
Second Prize: NPR 4,500 and a Certificate
Certificate of appreciation for 2nd and 3rd runner up.

$75 credit of AWS will be given to all the participants.


Rules and Regulations

Participants must agree upon the below mentioned rules and regulations:

  • Participant must bring their own laptop and charger.
  • There will be no internet connection during the competition.
  • Copy and pen for the participant will be provided.
  • For any issues arrived participants must agree upon the jury’s decision.
  • Collaboration among team member is expected but one team can’t collaborate with other teams during competition.

Deadline

The deadline for form submission is July 12, 2019.
